Your protections*


A Household Loan is a Reverse Mortgage, which is regulated product under the National Consumer Credit Protection ("NCCP") Act. There are specific contractual protections that apply to these types of loans including:

  • Ownership - you own your own home and remain registered on the title

  • Occupancy - you cannot be forced to leave your home (unless you are no longer able to live independently)

  • No Negative Equity Guarantee - you cannot owe more than the value of your home

How we get paid


Interest is charged on capital drawn from your home equity and paid when you repay your loan.
