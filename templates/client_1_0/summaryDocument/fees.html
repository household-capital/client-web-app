Legal Protections


You own your own home and remain registered on the title. Your occupancy of your home is protected by Commonwealth legislation, including a statutory no Negative Equity Guarantee. You can never be forced to leave your home unless you are no longer able to live independently at home or commit a serious breach of contract. You can never owe more than the value of your home unless you commit a serious breach of contract.
