Thank you for completing an online application for a Home Income Loan.


We will organise a phone call with one of our Retirement Specialists to discuss your application, answer any questions and confirm that this loan is right for you. Following that call, we will organise a home valuation to be undertaken.


There are some additional steps that we need you to complete before we can assess your application. These steps are detailed below.


1. Verification of identity

We are required to verify the identity of all borrowers.


We use Australia Post to perform this verification on our behalf. Attached to this email is an identification form as well as instructions as to the type of forms of identification that you are required to provide. You will need to complete the verification process by taking the form and supporting documentation into an Australia Post branch. They will do the rest.


We also offer an online alternative to this service. Please contact us to arrange.


2. Expense verification

We require a copy of:

  • a council rates notice;
  • an insurance notice (Certificate of Currency or Annual Renewal);
  • a strata levies notice (if applicable); and
  • a credit card statement (for each card, front page only)

For the home related items, we need to be able verify the amount you are currently paying. For the credit cards, we need to be able to verify the current amount and limit.


You can upload a copy, scan or photo of these items using our document upload page. If you are on a mobile device, you can even take a picture of these items and send to us. Please use the following button to access this upload page.



Alternatively, you can email us this information to customers@householdcapital.com



We are here to help. If you have any questions or experience difficulties with any of these steps, please call us on 1300 622 100.
