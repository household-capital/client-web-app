  Household Loan

A Household Loan is a type of reverse mortgage, so you enjoy a number of flexible features and important customer protections


Features
  • You are not required to make periodic loan or interest repayments
  • Interest is also added to the loan balance on a monthly basis
  • As a result, the loan balance will grow over time
  • When you (or the last borrower) leave the home the loan balance at that point is repaid
  • You can repay the loan at anytime without penalty
Protections
  • Ownership - you continue to own your home and benefit from increases in value over time
  • Occupancy - you can remain in your home as long as you want to. The loan is repayable when you (or the last borrower) sell the home, leave the home or are unable to live independently
  • No Negative Equity Guarantee - you can never owe more under the loan than your house is worth
