How does a Household Transfer work?


A Household Transfer is our approach to borrowing against home equity for responsible, long-term, retirement funding. Our loan is structured as a reverse mortgage and therefore you benefit from strong legal protections


Key benefits

  • you remain the owner of your home
  • you can stay in your home as long as you want to
  • the loan is only repayable when you leave or sell your home
  • you cannot owe more than the house is worth

Key features

  • a low interest rate, currently  
  • no ongoing fees
  • no requirement to pay interest (although you can)
  • interest is added to the loan balance
  • you can repay the loan at any time, without penalty


How does this change over time?


As the interest on a Household Transfer is added to the principal (the borrowed amount), the loan balance grows over time. However, the value of your home may also grow over time
