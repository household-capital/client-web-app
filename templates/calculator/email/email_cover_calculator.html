How does a Household Transfer™ work?

A Household Transfer™ is our approach to accessing your home equity for responsible, long-term, retirement funding. A Household loan is a type of reverse mortgage, meaning you benefit from strong consumer legal protections.


Key benefits
  • you remain the owner of your home
  • you can stay in your home as long as you want to
  • the loan is only repayable when you leave or sell your home
